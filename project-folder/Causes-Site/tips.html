Tips for owning a rescued pet!

And for having a loving pet forever!

  • Figure out where your pet will stay and puppy/kitten proof it! Also, let your new pet get acclimated to his/her new surroundings. Start a schedule for feeding, walking, and bathroom breaks.
  • Make sure you buy food, bowls for water and food, a leash, collar, and bedding before you even bring the pet home.
  • Training should begin as soon as you bring your pet home, with teaching vocabulary and directions. This will help prevent confusion.
  • Be prepared for accidents, as they are bound to happen. You have to go with the flow.
  • Getting your pet microchipped is a new and popular way to track your pets with very little risks involved.
  • Last but not least make sure you find the right pet to fit your personality and mood.
  • If you follow these tips, you'll be on your way to having a happy and well-adjusted pet.
