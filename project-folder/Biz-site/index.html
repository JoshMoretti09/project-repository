Moretti's Food Truck

Established in 2015 in a musty Northeast Philadelphia basement by two brothers named Josh and Jeremy, who quickly took Philadelphia by storm with there interesting take on traditional Italian street food.

In the summer of 2015 the two brothers entered the food truck in the world championships of food trucking airing on ESPN 8 the 'Ocho' this fall. After taking home the top prize, the golden Food Truck, the brothers quickly became the pride of Philly second only to Rocky Balboa.
